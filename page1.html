Lisbon Sailing 

Sail the river Tagus and get a unique perspective of Lisbon 

BOOK NOW

Do you want to get away from the crowds?

Sail away with Eva Luna and be prepared to create life lasting memories with our crew. "Me boat és su boat" and because of that we aim for more intimate and unique experiences, where you're the captain.
So, why not let yourself dive into the beauty of all things?!

Trip Details

First we will set from Doca do Espanhol in Alcântara, cruising along some of the most emblematic areas and monuments of Lisbon such as the downtown area, Cais do Sodré and Terreiro do paço with views that extend to Lisbon's Castle. Then, we route back west, sailing under Ponte 25 de Abril we are able to see some of the most important historic monuments and buildings in Lisbon- such as Cristo Rei, Maat, Torre de Belém and Padrão dos descobrimentos
